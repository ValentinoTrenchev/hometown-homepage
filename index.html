Visit Skopje

Enjoy an incredible mix of history, culture, and natural beauty that make it a perfect destination for any traveler.

Top three activities to do at Skopje

Matka Canyon

Matka Canyon is surrounded with mountains, great spots for photographers. beautiful nature and breathtaking landscapes.

Stone Bridge

Cross the Vardar River by the Stone bridge, the only preserved bridge from the Middle ages and start our exploration of the new part of the city.

Old Bazaar

Take a short walk to the old bazaar, a place steeped in history. Different lanes have different specialties and skills. A host of nice restaurants await too!
